Design, Innovation and Society, Studio 3 coursework. Students were prompted to design a physical product for the DIS studio space. Our studio sessions were three hours long: 2 hours lecture, 1 hour in-studio project time. I found myself becoming tired of the space I was in after the long lecture period and this feeling inspired me to dig deeper into how I could help students effectively use their class time in the studio. I went the experimental route and set out to develop a device that would inspire and reset the mind of a tired student after lecture. The product report is the culmination of my research and development over an 8-week period. Drawings were completed in Adobe Illustrator and the report was compiled using InDesign.
